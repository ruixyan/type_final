SYMBIOSIS

/INFO


SYMBIOSIS was developed to explore our (humanity's) relationship with technology. It utilizes a distortion axis to create 6 different styles: starting with a vectorization of my handwiting and ending with some loose semblance of a letterform.

/DIST


Utilizing perlin noise as a method of distortion, I displaced the letterforms, and used a pixel grid to keep the resolution of the letterforms crisp for vectorization. Perlin noise is a common method of distortion used for generating noise, and also landforms (the terrain generated in Minecraft uses perlin noise!) It is also featured in the hero section above, where you can hover over it to interact.
