/About/Typeface

This font was created to explore the relationship between technology and human design. Also to explore different methods of creating interfaces utilizing computer vision, quite literally putting human gesture in tangent with the generation of the letterforms.

/About/Distortion

Utilizing perlin noise as a method of distortion, this font was created using my own imperfect handwriting - crazy slants and all - and p5js.

/About/Computer_Vision

Using MediaPipe, I encorporated computer vision into the the UI.
