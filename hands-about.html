/ABOUT

This font, SYMBIOSIS v1.0, was developed to explore our (humanityâ€™s) relationship with technology. It features 6 different styles of various levels of distortion: starting with a vectorization of my handwiting, and ending with some loose semblance of a letterform. I used p5js and perlin noise (see the hero above) to distort and pixelate the forms of my handwriting. Once the distorted letterforms were screenshotted, I took them into illustrator and Image Traced them, creating some pretty wonky pixel grids that I honestly liked more than I thought I would.

/DIST

Utilizing perlin noise as a method of distortion, I displaced the letterforms, and used a pixel grid to keep the resolution of the letterforms crisp for vectorization.
