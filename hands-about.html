/ABOUT

This font, SYMBIOSIS v1.0, was developed to explore our (humanity's) relationship with technology. It features 6 different styles of various levels of distortion: starting with a vectorization of my handwiting, and ending with some loose semblance of a letterform. I used p5js and perlin noise (see the hero above) to distort and pixelate the forms of my handwriting. Once the distorted letterforms were screenshotted, I took them into illustrator and Image Traced them, creating some pretty wonky pixel grids that I honestly liked more than I thought I would.

/DIST

Utilizing perlin noise as a method of distortion, I displaced the letterforms, and used a pixel grid to keep the resolution of the letterforms crisp for vectorization. Perlin noise is a common method of distortion used for generating noise, and also landforms (the terrain generated in Minecraft uses perlin noise!) It is also featured in the hero section above, where you can hover over it to interact.
